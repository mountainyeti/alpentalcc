Member News and Announcements

ACC 2018 Winter Meeting

December 1st, 2018 2:00-4:00pm
Snoqualmie Pass Fire Station

The winter meeting will be on December 1st. We will have it from 2-4PM so as to not conflict with the Firefighters ball. We will have an 30 minute social (no alcohol per fire department rules). Then start the meeting at 2:30. If people wish to bring light finger foods, that would be appreciated.

Water Update

This is a friendly neighborhood reminder. Our water utility district is awaiting your input to continue and finalize the process of repairing our critical ACC water system. This is part of the requirement from the Department of Health to turn off the chlorine in the water. I have attached a copy and there is a copy on the SPUD website. Lets all do our part so we can get the chlorine turned off.

A copy of the control survey can be found here.

Water issues or Questions? For more information, please call Snoqualmie Pass Utility District at (425)434-6600 or email: customercare@snopass.org

Reminders:

A reminder for all homeowners to remind your contractors to clean up all debris before the snow hits. If debris left out damages snow plow equipment again this year, ACC bill them for the damage. Also, please make plans for dealing with the snow and parking. There will be NO exceptions for parking on ACC roads. Parking is available at Sahalie during the week and west on the weekends. The area past Sahalie before Ober Strasse will not be available for our use this year. Sahalie will tow if people try to park there.
