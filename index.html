Alpental Community Club

A Tyrolean-Bavarian community in the Cascades


Scroll Down


Alpental Community Club, Inc., is a nonprofit corporation that was formed in 1968 for the purposes of furnishing and regulating road maintenance, snow removal services, and such other services and facilities for the benefit of the owners and residents of our mountain community.
