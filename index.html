Dues Mailing

We are noticing that the dues payment returns are coming in at an unusually slow rate. Also, some residents have informed us that they have not received the most recent billing. Therefore, our ACC Treasurer, Dave Harja, is going to send out a second bill to all of us that have yet to pay our most recent quarterly dues. A special thanks to those of you that gave us a "heads up" about not receiving your bill as that information helped us to discover that there is some issues involved. We expect the second mailing should be to you by May 24th.

Fire Protection Information

We have added information for defending your home from wildfires to the archive. Please take a look and make sure your home is protected from fires. Not only will following these principles protect your own home, but will help protect your neighbors as well.

Every year, wildfires burn across the U.S., and more and more people are living where wildfires are a real risk. Nearly 45 million homes abut or intermingle with wildlands and more than 72,000 U.S. communities are now at risk. But by working together residents can make their own property - and their neighborhood - much safer from wildfire. - National Fire Protection Agency

Alpental Community Club, Inc., is a nonprofit corporation that was formed in 1968 for the purposes of furnishing and regulating road maintenance, snow removal services, and such other services and facilities for the benefit of the owners and residents of our mountain community.
