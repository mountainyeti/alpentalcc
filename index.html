Winter Meeting

December 7th, 2019 at 3:30pm
Snoqualmie Pass Fire Station

NEW TIME for ACC Winter Meeting is 3:30 pm on Saturday, December 7th at the Snoqualmie Pass Fire Station. The agenda will be focused on our financials, a dues increase proposal to save money for road improvements and election of a new Secretary. We hope to see you there!

4th Quarter Dues

Due November 14, 2019

Remember that 4th quarter dues are coming due soon. You will have 30 days to pay from the due date of November 14th, 2019. A reminder will be sent on the 45th day past the due date. At day 60 days a $25 late fee will be applied to your account for each quarter it is late. At 180 days we will start charging interest for anything over 180 days. If you do not indicated on your payments which quarter it is for, we will apply it to the oldest quarter owed.

Please use the new PO box for your payments:

Alpental Community Club, Inc., is a nonprofit corporation that was formed in 1968 for the purposes of furnishing and regulating road maintenance, snow removal services, and such other services and facilities for the benefit of the owners and residents of our mountain community.
