Q3 Dues

This is a reminder that 3rd Quarter dues were due on August 15th, 2020. If you have not paid, please be sure to send payment asap. You will receive a reminder from the Treasurer at 45 days due, and after 60 days due late fees will be assessed.

Net Due: 30 Days
Past Due Notice: 45 Days
Late Fees: 60 Days ($25/Quarter)

Alpental Community Club, Inc., is a nonprofit corporation that was formed in 1968 for the purposes of furnishing and regulating road maintenance, snow removal services, and such other services and facilities for the benefit of the owners and residents of our mountain community.

Alpental Community Club Board

Adam Cooper

President

I found home in the Alpental valley in 2011, and I am blessed to build memories on the snow, trails, and around the table with my wife and children. Surrounded by trees, mountains, family and friends, I am truly living my dream. Whether it’s snowskating Alpental in the winter paddling a mountain lake in the summer, or picking huckleberries in the fall, I always find a way to get a smile on my face around here...

Dave Harja
